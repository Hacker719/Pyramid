How to play Pyramid

Instructions

In the game you alternate turns between two people. In a turn, you can cross out as many lines as you want in one row. You can start from any line and go backwards or forwards but ONLY HORIZONTAL. You both keep alternating until there are no lines left. The last person who played loses.
